Our Mission:

M.A.R.S which stands for Marine Aquatic Research Sources is a non-profit organization aiming to help out highschool and college by providing them with community service opportunities while simultaneously helping out marine life environment by cleaning up beaches, educating students on marine life and spreading awareness, traveling across oceans and lakes in hopes of making our oceans and lakes to make them cleaner and safer for both humans and marine animals alike.

During our trip to hawaii we stumbled upon a green turtle struggling to get a plastic ring from off of its neck, as tourist we were astonished hawaii is known as one of the world's most beautiful, nature friendly places of all time has animals that were not safe from our pollution, it open our eyes as we saw the extent of pollution can do. From that day forward we decided to devote ourselves to improving the environment as much as we could.
